# Epics
## Purpose

This document defines the high-level areas of functionality for the Kanban application.

Epics represent large parts of the system and are used to organize features.

---

## Epic 1: Board Management

Large area of functionality focused on creating, viewing, and managing Kanban boards. Users should be able to initialize a new board and see a ready-to-use interface.

---

## Epic 2: Column Organization

Large area of functionality focused on organizing workflow stages. Users should be able to create, rename, and manage columns that represent different phases of work.

---

## Epic 3: Task Management

Large area of functionality focused on creating and managing individual task cards. Users should be able to quickly add, edit, and remove work items from the board.

---

## Epic 4: Card Organization

Large area of functionality focused on organizing and prioritizing cards within and across columns. Users should be able to move and reorder cards to reflect status and priority.

---

## Epic 5: Board Persistence

Large area of functionality focused on saving and retrieving board state. Users should be able to close and return to the application without losing work.
